The Ronin

About

You are a Cyborg Samurai who wants to take revenge on the ones who tried to kill you before: The Robot Shogunate

Your ultimate objective is to exact revenge on the alien who left you scarred and deformed. He is the final boss and so you have to clear the levels leading up to it. Slash and destroy all who stand in your way.

You might be the only one without a gun, but fear not, for your cybernetic form allows you to deflect all laser projectiles fired your way.

Developed on the Unity Game Engine.

Role: Team Lead, Sound Designer, Programmer

Developed in a team of 3 over the course of 15 weeks.
