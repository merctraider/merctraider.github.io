Yokai Parade

About

Alyse is under attack and she needs your help!

Get ready to slice and smite the parades of Yokai invading the gate to the human realm!

Struggling to hold down the fort? Collect the coins earned from your service to unlock the many Powerups and Charms to take down the toughest of the Yokai. Use these at your disposal to form powerful combinations and strategies and you can take on even legendary monsters!

Developed on the Unity Game Engine.

Role: Team Lead, Sound Designer, Programmer

Developed in a team of 7 over the course of 15 weeks
